What is AstroX?



AstorX is an application that will provide an intelligent fight detection system that will alert the user of any violent and aggressive actions that could occur, using a surveillance camera that is linked to the website. Our system will use computer vision and deep learning technologies to detect any fights. Our main goal is to maintain pubic safety and to minimize the attacks as well as the response time for authorities to assist. Our system will target public spaces such as schools, universities, prisons and malls.
